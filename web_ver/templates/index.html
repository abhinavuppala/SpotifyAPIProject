How to play

Song Hangman Rules

In this game, you play hangman, except you are trying to guess a random song made by your chosen artist.

Hangman Rules

You take turns guessing either a letter/number that might be in the song, or the entire song name. If the letter/number is in the song name, all occurences of that character are revealed. If not, the "hangman" gets closer to being hung. To win, guess the full song name before the hangman is completed.
